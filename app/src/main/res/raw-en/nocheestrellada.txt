Title : The Starry Night
Artist : Vincent van Gogh
Year : 1889
Movement : Post-Impressionist
Location : Museum of Modern Art in Manhatan


The Starry Night is a painting painted in 1889 by the Dutch artist Vincent van Gogh (1853-1890). It is considered one of the most important paintings by the painter for its unique and original style, which marked a turning point in post-impressionist art.
